### Linear velocity
$$v=\frac{s}{t}$$
Where s the length of the arc and t is the time. 

### Angular velocity
$$\omega =  \frac{\theta}{t}$$

### Relating linear and angular velocity
$$v=\frac{r\theta}{t} = r\frac{\theta}{t} = r\omega$$
$$v=r\omega$$
$$\omega=\frac{v}{r}$$
